What is BeatBounceValues?

BeatBounceValues is a values assessment test designed for the Beat Bounce moderator application. This test presents a series of statements, and you will respond with your opinion, ranging from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree. Each answer will slightly affect your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a compass, helping to determine your core values.

There are questions in the test. Please answer honestly.
